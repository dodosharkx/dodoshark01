 Challenges in Raw Material Processing

 Oil-rich legumes: The grinding process generates heat and releases oil, leading to screen clogging

 Whole grains with husks: High fibre toughness makes it difficult for standard equipment to grind them uniformly

 High fineness requirements: Food-grade applications often require a mesh size of 100 or higher

 Strict hygiene standards: The food and pharmaceutical industries require stainless steel contact parts
