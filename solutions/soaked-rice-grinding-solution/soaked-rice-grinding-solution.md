High-efficiency Rice Milling Solution Using DoDoShark

 Abstract

 [Image of Soaked Rice]

This solution is specifically tailored to meet the high-efficiency milling requirements for soaked rice (wet rice), addressing the industry challenges associated with traditional equipment, such as clogging, screen fouling and low throughput when processing wet materials.Leveraging three core technologies—stainless steel construction, a widened chamber structure and a high-volume air-cooling system—DoDoShark ensures stable processing of soaked rice. It offers selectable mesh sizes ranging from 0.4 to 0.8 mm (fineness approximately 80–120 mesh), with a maximum hourly output of over 3 tonnes per unit.The solution features a fully dust-free design covering the entire process from feeding and grinding to collection, ensuring food-grade hygiene standards. It helps rice product processing enterprises (producing rice cakes, rice noodles, rice vermicelli, tangyuan flour, etc.) to increase production capacity, reduce the frequency of downtime for cleaning, and stabilise product quality.
